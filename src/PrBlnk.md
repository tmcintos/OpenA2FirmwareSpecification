### <a id="prblnk-f948"></a>PrBlnk ($F948)

**Description:**

This routine prints three blank spaces to standard output.

**Input:**

*   **Registers:**
    *   A: N/A
    *   X: N/A
    *   Y: N/A
*   **Memory:** None.

**Output:**

*   **Registers:**
    *   A: Undefined
    *   X: Preserved
    *   Y: Preserved
    *   P: Undefined
*   **Memory:**
    *   Standard output is used.

**Side Effects:**

*   Prints three blank spaces to standard output.
